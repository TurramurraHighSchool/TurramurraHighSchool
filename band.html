Turramurra High School

Band

At Turramurra High School we have an enviable reputation for outstanding musical achievement.

The comprehensive band program enables students to actively participate in a music program that delivers an enriched musical experience, enjoyment and a sense of teamwork.

The Turramurra High School Band Program has tremendous support from within our school community, from parents and others from within the general community. It is highly regarded and is widely recognised for outstanding commitment and achievement.
