Turramurra High School

Turramurra High School has a proud tradition of academic excellence. This is consistently reflected by our outstanding HSC results. We offer a wide variety of subject choices which supports all students to engage in their learning.

Our school caters to individual learning styles by providing innovative enrichment programs as well as successful learning support programs. All students are encouraged to aspire to achieve their personal best.

We foster in our students a passion for lifelong learning providing them with foundations for their future academic success. Our careers advice is second to none with school, parents and students work closely together to support a successful transition to the next stage of their life.

An active professional learning program ensures that staff are at the forefront of educational standards. Student learning is greatly enhanced by this ongoing enquiry and commitment to best professional/educational practice.

The diverse curriculum offered at Turramurra High School engages and stimulates students creating 21st century learners, celebrating and rewarding personal success. It is delivered by dedicated, expert teachers who engender a passion to academically extend all students.
