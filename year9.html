Turramurra High School

Year 9

Year 9 at Turramurra High School marks the beginning of the transition between the junior and senior high school years. Students are given the choice of three elective subjects allowing them to pursue their areas of interest and ability.

Turramurra High School offers a very broad curriculum with over twenty courses to choose from in Year 9 ranging from Languages to Engineering, Music to Software Design, Food Technology to Visual Arts. Turramurra High caters for every interest.
